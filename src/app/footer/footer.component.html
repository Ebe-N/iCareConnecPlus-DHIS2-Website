About Us

Started 21 years ago as a small-scale research project supported by the University of Oslo, whose initial implementation achievements in the Coast Region led to DHIS2 adoption by Tanzania's Ministry of Health. The pilot success in the Coast region acted as a catalyst to enable DHIS2's full-scale rollout in the health sector of Tanzania.
